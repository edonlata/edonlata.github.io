NCAA Basketball Trends & Team Insights

This project analyzes NCAA basketball team statistics to uncover scoring patterns, efficiency metrics, and factors that influence team success.

Steps I Took

  • Imported team & game stats into Excel.
  • Cleaned data + created fields like PPG, FG%, win rate.
  • Built pivot tables by conference & season.
  • Created charts connecting efficiency with wins.

Key Insights

  • Higher eFG% strongly correlates with win percentage.
  • Balanced offense + defense beats high scoring alone.
  • Free throws & turnovers decide close games.
  • Strength of schedule impacts team success.

Tools Used

Excel, pivot tables, data visualization.

Deliverables

  • Cleaned NCAA datasets
  • Pivot table insights
  • Data visualizations
  • Full presentation deck
